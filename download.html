How to Set Up Your Tablet for Installation

  1. Download the app by clicking the "Download App" button above.
  2. On your tablet, go to Settings > Apps & notifications > Special App Access > Install Unknown Apps.
  3. Select your browser (e.g., Chrome or Firefox) and enable Allow from this source.
  4. Open your Downloads folder, tap the APK file, and select Install.
  5. Once installed, you can find the app in your app drawer. Launch and enjoy!

Note: Only download the app from this official website to ensure security and authenticity.
